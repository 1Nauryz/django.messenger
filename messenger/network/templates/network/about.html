About Our Products

Messenger

1.3 billion people around the world use Messenger every month to message, video chat and plan things—all in one place.
The app makes it easy to message almost anyone — even without being friends on Facebook or having their phone
number. On Messenger anyone can show their reaction, share photos and videos, send money easily and securely*,
share their location, play games and much more. Beyond connecting with friends, it has also become a thriving channel
for brands, developers and consumers to connect with each other and the things they love.

*this feature is only available in certain countries

Messenger Kids

Messenger Kids is a free video calling and messaging app designed for kids to connect with close friends and family
from their tablet or smartphone. Group or one-on-one video calls with loved ones are more fun with interactive masks,
reactions and sound effects. Parents manage the contact list and decide who can connect with their children. Messages
don’t disappear and can’t be hidden in case parents would like to check in. Messenger Kids allows kids to message and
video call using Wi-Fi so they don’t need a phone number.


Leadership

Loredana Crisan

VP of Messenger

Loredana Crisan is the head of Messenger at Meta. In this role, Loredana leads her team in developing innovative experiences for Messenger’s more than one billion users and pushing the boundaries in modern-day communication. Previously, Loredana was the VP of Meta’s messaging experiences at Messenger and Instagram where she led the strategy and launch of the new Messenger experience on Instagram including cross-app communication between the two apps, and the introduction of new expression and safety features in Messenger’s end-to-end encrypted surfaces. She also led Messenger's design, brand and research teams who were responsible for Messenger’s redesign and rebranding in 2018 and 2020. Prior to Messenger, Loredana led brand and mobile design at Indiegogo where she spearheaded the release of Indiegogo's native apps as well as the launch of Indiegogo's charitable fundraising site, Generosity. Loredana grew up in Romania, and she studied piano and music composition at the National Music University in Bucharest.


Ime Archibong

VP of Product for Messenger

Ime Archibong is the VP of Product for Messenger, overseeing all teams responsible for building Messenger products, features, and experiences. Previously, Ime was Head of New Product Experimentation (NPE) at Meta, where he led a R&D organization responsible for testing new product bets for Meta. Prior to this, Ime served as Meta’s VP of Product Partnerships and his career began at IBM as a software engineer. He is a listed inventor on more than a dozen technical patents, holds a Bachelor’s of Science degree in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering from Yale University and an MBA from the Stanford Graduate School of Business. He serves on the board of Capital One and a few nonprofits focused on local community, education, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
